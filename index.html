BMI Calculator

What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measurement used to assess whether someone has a healthy weight based on their height and weight. It is a common indicator used by medical professionals to assess the health risks associated with someone's weight.


What is a BMI Calculator?

BMI calculator is a tool designed to determine whether your weight falls within the ideal category or not. It can be utilized by individuals aged 20 and above. However, it's essential to note that this BMI calculator cannot be applied to pregnant women and children due to differing BMI standards.


Things to pay attention to in the BMI Calculator:

  • The Normal BMI ranges between 18,5 and 24,9
  • If your BMI exceeds 25 it indicates excess weight
  • Conversely, if your BMI is below 17 it signifies low body weight
  • When your BMI reaches or exceeds 30, it's advisable to take preventive measures to avoid illness
